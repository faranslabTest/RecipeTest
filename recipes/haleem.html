Farans Haleem Recipe

Haleem is a beef lentil stew thats gas. nuff said

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Beef Chunks
  • Shaan Haleem Easy Cook Packet
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Some oil
  • Chilli Powder, Garam masala, Turmeric, Cumin, Coriander, Salt

Steps

  1. First step is to slow cook the beef for like 1 hour to 2 hours, depending on your cooker.
  2. Simultaneously, you should take the daals and let them soak
  3. Blend/Pulverize the softened meat to your preferred texture
  4. Add it all together in a pot and let cook until preferred consistency
